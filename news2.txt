The electrical installation and grounding system of this
appliance shall be performed by qualified people. Do not use
the neutral wire for grounding. This appliance can heat the water
over than suitable for shower. Adjusting the water 
temperature on the appliance shall be taken to prevent the risk of scalding.
In order to reduce the risk of injury, close supervision is 
necessary when the appliance is used by children,
elderly and people with reduced physical sensory or mental capabilities,
 or lack of experience and knowledge.
Do not use the product if you think it may be frozen.
